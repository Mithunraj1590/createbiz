Free Zone Company Formation in Dubai, UAE

Set up your company in one of the UAE’s renowned free zones. Whether you aim to trade, innovate, or provide services, free zones offer an unparalleled platform to thrive.

Why Set Up a Company in a Free Zone?

Free zones in the UAE are strategically designed to empower businesses with unique benefits and privileges, making them a preferred choice for entrepreneurs and corporations alike.

  • 100% Foreign Ownership: Retain complete control of your business without the need for a local sponsor.
  • Tax Benefits: Enjoy zero corporate and personal income tax, along with exemptions on import/export duties.
  • Ease of Doing BusinessBenefit from simplified licensing, visa processes, and company registration.
  • Strategic Locations: Free zones are situated near major ports, airports, and highways, ensuring global connectivity.
  • Ease of Scaling: Mainland companies enjoy flexibility for growth and expansion.
  • Sector-Specific Focus:Many free zones cater to specialized industries like technology, healthcare, media, and logistics, providing industry-specific incentives and facilities.

A free zone company is your gateway to a globally connected and financially advantageous business environment.

How to Choose the Best Free Zone for Your Business

With over 40 free zones in the UAE, selecting the right one can significantly impact your success. Consider the following factors:

  1. Nature of Your Business: Some free zones specialize in specific industries like media (Dubai Media City) or tech (Dubai Internet City).
  2. Location: Opt for free zones close to your target markets, ports, or airports for logistical efficiency.
  3. Facilities and Infrastructure: Assess the availability of office spaces, warehouses, and other facilities tailored to your business needs.
  4. Cost of Setup: Compare licensing fees, lease costs, and operational expenses to align with your budget.
  5. Future Growth: Choose a free zone that supports scalability and expansion, ensuring your long-term success.

Documents Required for Mainland Business Setup

To ensure a smooth registration process, prepare the following documents:

Download personalized Minland business setup checklist.
For Initial Approval
  • Copy of the passport for all partners or shareholders.
  • Copy of the UAE residence visa or entry stamp (if applicable).
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the current sponsor (if applicable).
For Trade Name and Licensing
  • Trade name reservation certificate.
  • Initial approval certificate.
  • MOA and/or Local Service Agent Agreement (if applicable).
  • Tenancy contract and Ejari (for Dubai).
For Additional Approvals
  • Specific approvals based on the business activity (e.g., Dubai Municipality, Ministry of Health, etc.).
For Visa Processing
  • Passport copies of employees.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Entry permit copies.
