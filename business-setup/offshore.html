Offshore Company Formation in Dubai, UAE

Start your global business operations with the advantages of UAE's offshore jurisdictions. Createbiz ensures a seamless setup for your offshore company.

Why Set Up a Business in Offshore UAE?

Offshore company formation in the UAE offers unmatched benefits for international entrepreneurs looking to expand globally while optimizing costs and taxes.

  • Tax Efficiency Offshore companies enjoy zero corporate and income tax.
  • Global Reach: Operate internationally with a UAE-registered offshore entity.
  • Privacy and Confidentiality:Maintain secure and confidential company ownership records.
  • Asset Protection: Safeguard your assets under UAE’s robust legal framework.
  • Ease of Operation: No requirement for a physical office or extensive staffing.

Whether you aim to streamline your operations or enter new markets, offshore company formation in the UAE provides a reliable platform for growth.

How to Select the Right Jurisdiction for Offshore Company Registration in Dubai

The UAE offers several offshore jurisdictions, each with unique benefits tailored to diverse business needs:

  • Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA) Ideal for global businesses seeking proximity to major ports and robust international trade frameworks.
  • Ras Al Khaimah International Corporate Centre (RAKICC): Known for cost-effective registration and straightforward compliance requirements.
  • Ajman Offshore:Perfect for small and medium enterprises aiming for cost savings and a streamlined setup.

At Createbiz, we assess your business needs and recommend the jurisdiction that best aligns with your goals and operational requirements.

Steps to Set Up an Offshore Company in UAE

Setting up your business in a free zone is a straightforward process with our expert guidance:

  1. Consultation and Planning
    • Define your business goals and requirements.
    • Select the appropriate jurisdiction with expert advice.
  2. Name Registration :
    • Choose a unique name that complies with the offshore authority’s guidelines.
  3. Register Your Company: Submit the application form, trade name, and required documents.
  4. Document Submission
    • Prepare and submit all required documents, including proof of identity and address for shareholders.
  5. Approval and Incorporation
    • Obtain initial approval and finalize the incorporation process with relevant authorities.
  6. Bank Account Setup
    • Open a corporate bank account to manage financial transactions.
  7. Certificate Issuance
    • Receive your offshore company license and certificate of incorporation.

Documents Required for Registering an Offshore Company in UAE

To ensure a seamless offshore company setup, prepare the following:

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement).
  • Bank reference letter for shareholders.
  • Detailed business plan (if required by the jurisdiction).
  • Memorandum and Articles of Association (MOA/AOA).
