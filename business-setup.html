Why Dubai is the best place
to Invest

Dubai is a global hub for business, innovation, and economic growth. Here’s why it stands out as the ultimate investment destination:

  • Strategic Location: Positioned at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa, Dubai offers unparalleled connectivity and access to global markets.
  • Business-Friendly Policies: With 100% foreign ownership in many sectors and zero personal or corporate income tax, Dubai creates a welcoming environment for investors.
  • World-Class Infrastructure: From cutting-edge transportation systems to state-of-the-art facilities, Dubai provides an ecosystem designed for growth.
  • Diverse Economy: Beyond oil, Dubai thrives in sectors like technology, tourism, real estate, healthcare, and logistics, offering a wide range of opportunities.
  • Expo Legacy: The legacy of Expo 2020 has bolstered Dubai’s reputation as a center of innovation, sustainability, and future-ready businesses.

Document needed for
Business Setup in UAE

The documentation required may vary depending on the jurisdiction and business type. Typically, you will need:

Passport Copies:

For all shareholders and managers.

Business Plan:

Outline of your business activities and objectives.

Proof of Address:

Valid residential address of shareholders.

No Objection Certificate (NOC)

For UAE residents, if applicable.

Power of Attorney:

If you’re appointing a representative.

Memorandum of Association (MOA):

Prepared with Createbiz’s guidance.
